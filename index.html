About

It’s been a while since we all came together and pulled off an event quite like the BarCamps from back around 2007. There’s a lot of folks in our community who didn’t get a chance to participate back then. It’s been seven years since the last BarCamp and, well, that’s a damn shame.

For the noobs:BarCamp is an ad-hoc unconference born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment. It is an intense event with discussions, demos, and interaction from attendees. (Read more, more, more)

  • Date: December 13, 2014
  • Where: Tech Wildcatters and Fort Work (211 N Ervay)
  • Who: Anyone willing to share and learn (see list below for attendees)

What BarCamp is.

  • Maximum community learning.
  • No Eventbrite, no fees, no promises.
  • When you come, be prepared to share with barcampers.
  • No spectators, only participants.

  • 6p - Midnight After Party

The Rules of BarCamp

  • 1st Rule: You do talk about BarCamp.
  • 2nd Rule: You do blog about BarCamp.
  • 3rd Rule: If you want to present, you must write your topic and name in a presentation slot.
  • 4th Rule: Only three word intros.
  • 5th Rule: As many presentations at a time as facilities allow for.
  • 6th Rule: No pre-scheduled presentations, no tourists.
  • 7th Rule: Presentations will go on as long as they have to or until they run into another presentation slot.
  • 8th Rule: If this is your first time at BarCamp, you HAVE to present. (Ok, you don't really HAVE to, but try to find someone to present with, or at least ask questions and be an interactive participant.)
